# Write PAPER Content

Write `RAW/PAPER/**/*.md` as paper-centered study notes.
Keep `SKILL_KR.md` synchronized whenever the substantive instructions in this file change.

## Focus

- Link the official paper source early in the body.
- Keep the document centered on the paper's question, method, result, and why it matters.
- If the paper depends on reusable theory that is not unique to the paper, link out to `THEORY` instead of re-teaching it in full.
- If the paper has code-related follow-up, link to `REPO` or `IMPLEMENT` rather than absorbing that material here.

## Preferred Structure

- official source
- problem setting
- core idea or algorithm
- what changed from prior work
- why the paper matters here
- links to nearby local notes

## Evidence Rules

- Prefer the actual paper PDF plus the official arXiv or publisher page.
- Separate direct paper claims from your interpretation.
- Do not use `tracked_versions` just to cite a paper.
